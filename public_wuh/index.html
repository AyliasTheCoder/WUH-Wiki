Welcome to the Wake Up Hate wiki!

Wake Up Hate is an American Metalcore band, formed by Jake Adkins (Vocals). He was later joined by Connor Riley (Guitar), Rufus Mann (Bass), and Matt Klein (Drums). They hit the scene with their debut album "[[Deep Sleep]](Albums/Deep_Sleep)", featuring songs "[[A New Way to Hate]]" and "[[Over the Edge]]"

They continued their adventure with singles "[[The Cure]]", "[[I Just Don't Love You Anymore]](IJDLYA Song)", and "[[The Panic]]", and released their album "[[I JUST DON'T LOVE YOU ANYMORE]](IJDLYA)" in 2022, featuring songs "[[Monster]]" and "[[Never Coming Back]]"
